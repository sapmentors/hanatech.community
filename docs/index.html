What is HANA Tech Con?

Get Connected! If there is anything about SAP HANA you always wanted to know but never dared to ask - this is the place for you! Be part of the community of development experts, users and partners to exchange, ignite and delight.

Get Involved! If you want to speak up in your own session worthwhile to be shared with this great community. Looking forward to see you there soon!

This year's event will take place in St. Leon-Rot (Germany) on July 10, 2025. Ticket reservation is not open yet. Follow us on social media to stay up-to-date!

Now we need your help!

  1. Propose Your Topic!

    Do you have a compelling idea or valuable expertise to share? Submit your topic proposal until March 10, 2025 and take the first step toward becoming a speaker at our conference. Showcase your unique insights and contribute to an inspiring event filled with thought-provoking discussions.

  2. Impress the Jury and Get Selected

    Our experienced jury will review all submissions with care, focusing on originality, relevance, and potential to engage the audience. The most impactful and innovative topics will be chosen to ensure a diverse and dynamic program. If your proposal is selected, you'll receive a formal invitation to join our roster of speakers.

  3. Join Us as a Speaker at the Conference

    Once selected, you'll have the opportunity to present your ideas to an audience eager to learn and connect. Take the stage, share your expertise, and spark meaningful conversations. This is your moment to inspire, grow your professional network, and leave a lasting impression on the community.

Early Bird Tickets

You have a long journey and need to plan your trip to this conference early (before the official registration starts)? For that, you need to complete a questionnaire form, which can be found here. The deadline to apply for an Early Bird Ticket is March 27, 2025, at 11:59 pm CEST.
